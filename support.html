How to Contribute

Follow these simple steps to share your designs with the KOMPONI community

1

Prepare Your Design

Create high-quality Figma components with proper naming, organization, and documentation.

2

Submit for Review

Send us your .fig file along with a brief description of your component and its use cases.

3

Quality Check

Our team reviews your submission for quality, uniqueness, and adherence to our guidelines.

4

Go Live

Once approved, your design goes live on KOMPONI with full attribution to you as the creator.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?
We primarily accept .fig files (Figma native format). Please ensure your components are well-organized with proper naming conventions.
Do I retain rights to my contributed designs?
Yes, you retain full rights to your original work. By contributing, you're granting a license for the community to use your designs for free.
How long does the review process take?
We typically review submissions within 1-2 business days. Complex submissions or those requiring feedback may take longer.
Can I update my contributed designs?
Absolutely! You can submit updates to your existing components anytime. Just email us with the updated file and mention it's an update.
